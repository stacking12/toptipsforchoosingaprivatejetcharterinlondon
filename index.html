Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Private Jet Charter and how does it work?

A private jet charter allows you to hire an entire aircraft exclusively for your group, on your own schedule. Unlike commercial aviation, there are no shared cabins, no fixed departure times, and no crowded airports. You simply contact us with your travel requirements — origin, destination, dates, and passenger count — and our team sources the most suitable aircraft, manages all flight logistics, and handles every detail so you can simply arrive and fly.

Which airports can I depart from in London for a Charter Flight?

London offers several excellent airports for charter flight departures, including London City Airport, Farnborough Airport, Luton Airport, Biggin Hill Airport, and Stansted Airport. These airports are popular with private aviation clients due to their exclusive FBO (Fixed Base Operator) terminals, expedited security processes, and convenient access. Our team will recommend the most suitable departure point based on your location and destination.

How much does a Private Jet Charter from London cost?

The cost of a private jet charter from London varies depending on the aircraft type, route, duration, and any additional services required. As a general guide, a light jet for a short European route may start from around £5,000 to £10,000 one way, while heavy jets for transatlantic flights can reach £60,000 or more. We provide fully transparent, all-inclusive quotes tailored to your specific journey — contact us for a bespoke estimate with no hidden charges.

How far in advance do I need to book a Private Charter?

One of the greatest advantages of private charter flying is flexibility. While we recommend booking as early as possible to guarantee aircraft availability and the best pricing, our team is experienced in arranging last-minute and same-day flights when required. We operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, and have an extensive network of operators that allows us to mobilise aircraft at very short notice for urgent travel needs across the UK and internationally.

Are Private Jet Charter Flights safe?

Absolutely. Safety is the foundation of everything we do at Private Jet Charter Flights London. We only work with operators who hold full approval from the UK Civil Aviation Authority (CAA) and relevant international aviation authorities. Every aircraft on our network undergoes rigorous maintenance checks, and all flight crews are fully licensed and regularly trained. We apply a thorough due-diligence process to each operator we partner with, ensuring every flight meets the very highest safety standards.
